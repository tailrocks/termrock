title: Quality migrations
---

## Display-column measurement

Widget geometry now measures Unicode display columns instead of scalar-value counts. CJK, combining marks, emoji sequences, and regional indicators no longer drift from painted hit regions.

This closes `WIDTH-HINT`, `WIDTH-LIST`, `WIDTH-STATUS`, and `WIDTH-ERROR`. Consumers need no API change; labels may wrap or reveal horizontal scrolling at different columns when their scalar count differs from their terminal width.

## Focused panel cue

Focused panels use a double border in addition to the semantic focus color. This makes focus observable without color perception while retaining caller style overrides.

This closes `FOCUS-PANEL`. Focused panel fixtures intentionally change from single-line to double-line border glyphs.
