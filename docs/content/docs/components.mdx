---
title: Components
---

Every component accepts consumer-owned labels and borrowed data. Keyboard routing is expressed as logical actions; focus and hover state remain explicit and stable IDs survive filtering and reordering.

- `ActionBar`: caller-defined actions and activation regions. Story `action-bar/basic`.
- `Backdrop`: backdrop without lifecycle ownership. Story `backdrop/basic`.
- `DetailTable`: label/value rows with copy/link capabilities. Story `detail-table/basic`.
- `Dialog`, `ChoiceDialog`, `MessageDialog`: shells and caller choices. Story `dialog/message`.
- `DiffView`: borrowed projected lines. Story `diff/basic`.
- `HintBar`: prioritized chord descriptors. Story `hint-bar/wrapped`.
- `List`: borrowed stable-ID rows. Story `list/selection`.
- `Panel`: semantic framing. Story `panel/focused`.
- `StatusBar`: caller-owned slots. Story `status-bar/basic`.
- `Tabs`: stable IDs, glyphs, and explicit state. Story `tabs/status`.
- `TextInput`: grapheme-safe editing and external validation. Story `text-input/filter`.
- `Toast`: severity and anchor metadata. Story `toast/success`.
- `Tree`: caller-flattened hierarchy, stable disclosure/selection IDs, explicit focus/loading/error rows, Page/wheel/scrollbar navigation, and painted hit regions. Story `tree/navigation`.

All widgets tolerate empty and narrow rectangles. Disabled items are not hit targets. Mouse regions derive from painted geometry. Consumers own navigation policy and dialog stacking.
